MD-102: Endpoint Administrator

Study resources for the Microsoft MD-102 certification — covering device deployment, Intune management, identity, and endpoint security.

The MD-102 certification validates your skills as a Microsoft 365 Endpoint Administrator. It covers deploying and managing devices using Microsoft Intune, Windows Autopilot, compliance policies, app deployment, and identity protection — skills directly relevant to modern EUC and IT support roles.

⚠️ Prerequisites recommended: It's helpful to have some hands-on experience with Intune or Windows management before sitting MD-102. AZ-900 or MS-900 knowledge is also useful but not required.
